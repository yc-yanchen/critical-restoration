Who are we

Our company, based in Toronto, is a top provider of damage restoration services, offering fast and effective solutions for any size emergency. With certified technicians and state-of-the-art technology, we guarantee exceptional customer service and complete satisfaction with every job. Trust us to restore your property after any water damage event.

Services

Fire Damage Restorations

Fire damage remediation restores fire-damaged property, including removal of damage, cleaning, repairing structural damage, and making the property safe and habitable again.

Read More

Flood Damage Restorations

Flood damage restoration involves removing standing water, drying out the affected area, cleaning and repairing structural damage to return a property to its pre-flood condition. It requires specialized equipment and training, and may involve working with insurance companies.

Read More

General Repairs

Our experienced technicians are equipped to handle any repair needs, from minor fixes to complex issues, ensuring your is in its best shape. Trust us for a hassle-free solution to all your repair problems.
